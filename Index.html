Autobiography

My passion for soccer sparked after a club soccer game in 2012; I missed two game-winning goals that would have brought my team to first place in the division. It was only after the game that I noticed how heavily I was depended on, and yet I failed to meet the expectations of not only my team, but also the spectators. I saw people who I had never seen before cheer me on at a mere recreational soccer game, making me long for further support. Ever since, my desire to improve as an individual not only applies to soccer, but also as a reliable person in my community. I strove to be someone who can contribute and impact a person's life, so I became more interactive and learned how amazing it felt to make strong bonds with others. Since then, I have received constant support, motivating me to accomplish many goals that I would have never been able to achieve alone in the past.
